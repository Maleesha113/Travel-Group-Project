Let's Discover The Sri Lanka



About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in South Asia. It is located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, and southeast of the Arabian Sea.

Sri Lanka has a rich cultural heritage and a diverse population. The country's history dates back to over 2,500 years, and it has been influenced by various cultures and religions over the centuries, including Buddhism, Hinduism, Islam, and Christianity.

Whether you're looking for beautiful beaches, ancient ruins, scenic mountains, or exotic wildlife, Sri Lanka has something to offer every type of traveler.





Best Places to visit in Sri Lanka

Kandy

Home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic, Kandy is one of the most sacred places for Buddhists in Sri Lanka. It's also a great base for exploring the nearby tea plantations and national parks.

Learn more

Ella

Located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, Ella is known for its stunning scenery and great hiking trails. Don't miss the Nine Arch Bridge and Little Adam's Peak.

Learn more

Sigiriya

Climb to the top of Lion Rock to see the ancient fortress of Sigiriya, which dates back to the 5th century. The view from the top is breathtaking.

Learn more

Galle

Located on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka, Galle is a historic city known for its Dutch colonial architecture and the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort.
